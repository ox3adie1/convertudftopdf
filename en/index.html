What is a UDF File?

UDF (UYAP Document Format) is a proprietary file format used by Turkey's national judiciary informatics platform, UYAP. All legal documents created within the UYAP Document Editor are saved in this format with a .udf extension.

The challenge? UDF files can only be opened with the UYAP Document Editor, which is only available on computers connected to the UYAP system. This means lawyers, legal professionals, and citizens often cannot view these documents outside the courthouse.

Solution: convertudftopdf.com converts UDF files to universally readable PDF format, directly in your browser. No software installation required, no files uploaded to any server.

About UYAP

UYAP (Ulusal Yargi Agi Platformu / National Judiciary Informatics System) is Turkey's comprehensive digital judiciary platform, developed and maintained by the Ministry of Justice. Launched in the early 2000s, it connects all courts, prosecutors' offices, enforcement offices, prisons, and notaries across Turkey.

UYAP serves over 200,000 judicial professionals and provides access to 85+ million citizens through its various portals. It is widely regarded as one of the most comprehensive e-justice systems in the world.
